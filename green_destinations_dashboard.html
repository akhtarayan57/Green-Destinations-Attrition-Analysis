Age Risk

Employees aged 18–24 have a 39.2% attrition rate — nearly 4× the rate of those aged 35–44 (10.1%). Early-career retention is the top priority.

💰

Income Impact

Those earning <$3,000/mo leave at 28.6%, while top earners ($15k+) leave at just 3.8%. A $2,046/mo income gap separates leavers from stayers on average.

📅

Tenure Effect

The first year is the most critical — 34.9% leave within 0–1 years. Attrition drops to 8.1% for employees with 10+ years. Strong onboarding saves talent.
